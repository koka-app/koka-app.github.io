Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 8, 2026

You can delete your Koka account directly from the app. Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from our servers.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Koka app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom-right).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm by re-authenticating with your Google account.
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted.

What gets deleted

  • Account information — your Google display name, email, and profile photo are removed from Firebase Authentication.
  • Cloud data — all baskets and associated data stored in Firebase Firestore are permanently deleted.
  • Purchase link — your RevenueCat purchase record is unlinked from your account.
  • Pro status — your Koka Pro entitlement is reset on the device.

What is NOT deleted

  • Local data — preferences (region, theme, tab visibility) stored on your device via SharedPreferences remain until you uninstall the app.
  • App store purchase records — Apple App Store and Google Play maintain their own purchase history, which Koka cannot modify.
  • Aggregate analytics — anonymized, non-identifiable usage metrics collected via Firebase Analytics are retained.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account data cannot be recovered. If you purchased Koka Pro, you can restore your purchase by signing in again and tapping "Restore Purchases" on the upgrade screen.
