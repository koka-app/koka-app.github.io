2. Description of Service

Koka is a smart shopping companion that helps Filipino consumers:

  • Browse real-time market prices from DA Bantay Presyo across 17 Philippine regions
  • Build shopping baskets with budget tracking and actual spend capture
  • Get Filipino recipe suggestions with ingredient price lookups
  • Interact with a rule-based AI assistant for price queries, meal suggestions, and budget tracking
  • Auto-detect your nearest region via GPS for local price data

The App is available as a free tier (with ads) and a paid upgrade (Koka Pro).

3. Koka Pro

Koka Pro is a one-time lifetime purchase (₱149) available through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. It unlocks:

  • Full Koka AI assistant (price checks, recipe suggestions, budget management, and food jokes)
  • Unlimited baskets (free tier: 1 active basket)
  • Ad-free experience
  • Spending history

Pro is tied to your Google account (Firebase UID), not to a specific device. You must be signed in to purchase and to access Pro features. Signing out resets Pro status on the device; signing back in restores it.

Refunds are handled by the respective app store per their refund policies.
